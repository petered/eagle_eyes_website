Transport Canada Advanced RPAS Flight Review

Already passed the Advanced exam? A certified reviewer takes you through the mandatory in-person flight review, the last step before you apply for your Advanced Operations certificate, and submits your results to Transport Canada for you.

What it covers
  • Assessment by a certified Transport Canada flight reviewer
  • Review of your documentation and aircraft registration
  • Practical evaluation of flight planning, procedures, handling, and post-flight
  • Results submitted to the Drone Management Portal on your behalf
What to bring
  • A passed Advanced RPAS exam and valid government-issued ID
  • Drone Management Portal account access
  • A registered RPA (250 g to 25 kg). Bring your own, or we can rent you one. Reach out to inquire.
$250CAD / person
