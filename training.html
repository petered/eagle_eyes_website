Advanced Transport Canada RPAS License & Eagle Eyes Operator Day

A complete weekend: earn your Advanced RPAS license and become a confident Eagle Eyes operator. Starts with a focused online session on the Air Law portion of the Advanced exam, then moves into presentations, the exam itself, flight reviews, and a full operator day in the field.

Schedule
  • Online (pre-course): 4-hour session on the Air Law section of the Advanced License
  • Friday 6–9 PM: Advanced License presentation
  • Saturday 8 AM–12 PM: Advanced presentation  ·  12–2 PM: Advanced License exam
  • Saturday 2–4 PM: Flight reviews
  • Saturday 10 PM–midnight (optional): Night search exercise
  • Sunday 9 AM–4 PM: Eagle Eyes Operator Day: 2 hours theory, field time for the rest of the day
Includes
  • One Eagle Eyes license
  • Transport Canada Advanced RPAS flight review
  • In-person, hands-on instruction and real flight time in the field
