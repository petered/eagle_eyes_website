Completing the form on eagleeyessearch.com/caltopo also generates a code. Click the Copy code button there, then paste it below.

Step 1: Open CalTopo

On your computer, open CalTopo and follow these steps:

  1. Click your username in the top-right
  2. Click "Administer" next to your team name
  3. Go to the "Details" tab
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Create Service Account"
Back

Step 2: Fill in the details

Enter a title for the service account below, then type the same title into CalTopo. Make sure Update is selected as the permission, then click Create.

Back

Step 3: Enter the Credential Secret

Copy the secret from CalTopo and paste it here. It's only shown once!

Back

Step 4: Enter Account ID and Credential ID

Close the credential secret popup. Find the service account you just created in the list and enter the Account ID and Credential ID shown to the right of it.
