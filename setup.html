Connect your CalTopo account

Without a service account, your CalTopo session on the controller expires every few days and you'll need to log in again. A service account keeps your team permanently connected — set it up once and everyone on your license can use it.

You'll need admin access to your CalTopo team to set this up.

Step 1: Open CalTopo

On your computer, open CalTopo and follow these steps:

  1. Click your username in the top-right
  2. Click "Administer" next to your team name
  3. Go to the "Details" tab
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Create Service Account"
