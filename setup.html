CalTopo Service Account

How to create a service account:
  1. Open CalTopo, click your username, click "Administer" next to your team name
  2. Navigate to the "Details" tab
  3. Click "Create a Service Account" near the bottom
  4. Choose a title and permission level, click Create
  5. Immediately copy the Account ID, Credential ID, and Credential Secret — the secret is only shown once!
  6. Paste them into the fields below and click "Verify & Connect"
