CalTopo Service Account Setup

Create a CalTopo service account so Eagle Eyes stays permanently connected.

Create a Service Account in CalTopo

Open a new browser window and put it side by side with this one so you can easily switch back and forth.

In the new browser window, go to caltopo.com and sign in. You'll need admin access to your team. Then:

  1. Click your username in the top-right
  2. Click "Administer" next to your team name
  3. Go to the "Details" tab
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Create Service Account"
  5. Enter a title (e.g. "Eagle Eyes"), make sure Update is selected as the permission, then click Create

Enter the Credentials

After clicking Create, CalTopo shows a secret. Paste it here — it's only shown once!

Close the credential secret popup. Find the service account you just created in the list and enter the Account ID and Credential ID shown to the right of it.

Done! Transfer to your controller

If you got here from activating an Eagle Eyes license on your phone, go back to your phone and tap "Scan QR Code" to proceed.
