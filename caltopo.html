CalTopo Service Account Setup

Create a CalTopo service account so Eagle Eyes apps can stay permanently connected to CalTopo without needing to log in again.

Step 1: Create a Service Account in CalTopo

To set up a service account, you will need admin access to your CalTopo Teams account.
Open a new browser window and go to caltopo.com. Put it side by side with this one so you can easily switch back and forth. Then:

  1. Click your username in the top-right
  2. Click "Administer" next to your team name
  3. Go to the "Details" tab
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Create Service Account"
  5. Enter a title (e.g. "Eagle Eyes"), make sure Update is selected as the permission, then click Create

Step 2: Enter the Credentials

After clicking Create, CalTopo shows a secret. Paste it here — it's only shown once!

Close the credential secret popup. Find the service account you just created in the list and enter the Account ID and Credential ID shown to the right of it.

Step 3: Access URL (optional) NEW

Paste a CalTopo Access URL to unlock CalTopo's dedicated drone integration. Your drone appears on the shared CalTopo map with its live position, heading, altitude, and camera field of view — and anyone following along in CalTopo can click the drone's pin to watch the Eagle Eyes livestream for it, right from the map.

Where to find it: on caltopo.com, open the admin menu → Trackable Devices → Create new Access URL, then paste it here.

Double-check the paste — we can't verify this automatically yet.
