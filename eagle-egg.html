Flagship Field Kit

The Eagle Egg

A complete air-operations command post in a single rugged, waterproof case. Open the lid and your team is ready to fly — anywhere, online or off.

The Eagle Egg packs everything a search team needs to run air operations into one battery-powered, self-contained case. There's nothing to wire up and nothing to carry separately: open it on the hood of a truck, in a command tent, or on a trailhead, and start operating.

What's inside
  • Monitor built into the case lid — open it up and operate
  • Onboard battery for true standalone, self-powered use
  • Eagle Eyes RID receiver and ADS-B receiver built in
  • Onboard computer running Eagle Eyes Mirada for air ops, live detection, and in-field post-processing
  • Operates fully offline or online
▣

Lid-mounted monitor

A full display built into the case lid. Open it up and you're operating — no setup, no external screen.

⚡

Battery powered

Fully self-contained and self-powered, so your crew can run air ops with no mains power anywhere in the field.
