Licenses & credits

Purchase / Renew

Account owners control the money and licenses tied to this account. They can purchase and renew licenses, manage streaming credits, view billing history, and add or remove other owners.

Not the same as organization members. Being an admin or member of one of your organizations does not grant any billing authority on this account, and adding someone as an account owner does not place them in any organization. To manage who is on a team, go to Organization › People in the sidebar.

Admins

+ Add another account owner

Enter the email of the person you want to promote. They must already have an Eagle Eyes account at that address. They get owner powers immediately (purchase, billing, add / remove other owners).

About the account owner

The account owner is responsible for everything financial on this Eagle Eyes account. Account ownership is independent of organization roles (admin / member).

Account owners can:
  • Purchase and renew licenses
  • Buy streaming credits and subscribe to Annual Unlimited
  • Add and remove organizations from the account
  • See billing history and manage payment method
  • Add another account owner

To add or remove account owners, use the controls next to the owners list on this page.

People

See who's on the team and who manages it. Roles control what each person can do.

View roles and permissions
Admin
  • Manage every team the account funds: add and remove Members
  • Assign Managers and other Admins
  • Purchase & renew licenses, streaming credits, Unlimited
  • See billing history and invoices
Manager
  • Add and remove Members
  • Promote and demote Managers
  • Everything a Member can do
  • No billing access (unless also an Admin)
Member
  • Activate licenses and run the apps
  • Manage devices (disconnect / transfer)
  • Edit the team's shared configurations
