Module 7

Field Operations

⏱️ 90 minutes

Learning Objectives

  • Complete a comprehensive pre-flight checklist
  • Respond to in-flight emergencies
  • Execute common SAR workflows

10.1 Pre-Flight Checklist

Equipment

  • ☐ Drone battery >90%
  • ☐ Controller battery >90%
  • ☐ Phone/tablet battery >80%
  • ☐ SD card installed, space available
  • ☐ Propellers secure
  • ☐ Lens clean, cover removed

Eagle Eyes

  • ☐ App launches without errors
  • ☐ License active
  • ☐ Detection model loaded
  • ☐ Offline maps loaded for area
  • ☐ CalTopo connected
  • ☐ Device name set

Environment

  • ☐ Weather within limits
  • ☐ Wind <20 mph
  • ☐ Airspace authorized
  • ☐ No hazards (power lines, etc.)

10.2 Emergency Procedures

Low Battery

BatteryAction
30%Plan return route
25%Begin return
20%RTH recommended
15%Auto RTH may trigger

Lost Signal

  • Don't panic - drone continues flying
  • Move to higher ground
  • Wait for drone to execute RTH

AirSense Alert Enhanced in 1.6.0

DJI AirSense detects nearby manned aircraft via ADS-B transponders. Eagle Eyes 1.6.0 adds risk-based progressive alerts.

📷

Screenshot: AirSense aircraft alert with info panel

Aircraft Information

When an aircraft is detected, Eagle Eyes shows:

  • Aircraft call sign (if available)
  • Altitude and heading
  • Distance from your drone
  • Relative altitude (above/below you)

Response Procedure

  1. Check the alert - Note aircraft direction and altitude
  2. Assess relative altitude - Is it above, below, or at your level?
  3. Descend if needed - Reduce altitude to create separation
  4. Maintain visual - Watch the aircraft until clear
  5. Report - Inform team of manned traffic
  6. Resume - Continue mission when clear

AirSense Controls (1.6.0)

From the AirSense panel:

  • Mute - Silence alerts for a specific aircraft
  • Hide - Remove aircraft from map display
  • Info - View detailed aircraft data
💡
Don't mute all aircraft alerts. Only mute specific aircraft that are not a factor (e.g., high-altitude traffic).
⚠️
Not all aircraft have ADS-B transponders. Maintain visual awareness even with no alerts.
🚨
In ATTI mode (GPS lost), the drone will drift with wind. Land carefully as soon as possible.

10.3 SAR Search Workflow

Phase 1: Setup

  1. Brief with team on search area
  2. Load offline maps
  3. Import search boundaries (KML)
  4. Connect CalTopo
  5. Complete pre-flight checklist

Phase 2: Search

  1. Take off and gain altitude (60-100m)
  2. Fly systematic pattern (grid/sector)
  3. Monitor detections
  4. Investigate promising alerts
  5. Report finds to ground team

Phase 3: Recovery

  1. Return at 25-30% battery
  2. Land safely
  3. Swap battery if continuing
  4. Review detections
  5. Debrief findings
