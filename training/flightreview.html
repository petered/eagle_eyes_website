About the flight review

During the review, a certified RPAS Flight Reviewer will assess your ability to plan, brief, and safely conduct an RPAS flight in accordance with Transport Canada requirements. The session includes a review of required documents, site assessment, emergency procedures, pre-flight checks, flight operations, and post-flight procedures.

After the review, your result is submitted through Transport Canada's Drone Management Portal. If successful, you can apply for your Pilot Certificate – Advanced Operations through Transport Canada. Transport Canada says pilots must pass both the Advanced exam and an in-person flight review before applying for the Advanced certificate.

What's included

  • In-person Advanced RPAS flight review
  • Assessment by a certified RPAS Flight Reviewer
  • Pre-flight planning and documentation review
  • Practical flight assessment
  • Post-flight debrief
  • Submission of flight review result to Transport Canada

Before booking, you must have

  • Passed the Transport Canada Advanced RPAS exam
  • A registered RPAS suitable for the review
  • Your Drone Management Portal / pilot certificate information
  • Required documentation for the aircraft and operation
